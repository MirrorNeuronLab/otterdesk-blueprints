# Contract Clause Retrieval Playbook

## Useful Retrieval Queries

- Which source clauses support each extracted term, obligation, deadline, or risk?
- Which required clause categories are missing, ambiguous, conflicting, or outside the supplied taxonomy?
- What should counsel review before relying on the packet?

## Evidence Checklist

Tie every clause finding to contract text, page, heading, section number, or OCR snippet. Do not infer legal obligations from title, filename, or boilerplate expectations. If a clause is absent, mark it as "not found in supplied text" rather than "not applicable."

Review termination, renewal, assignment, confidentiality, liability cap, indemnity, payment, service levels, data protection, audit rights, governing law, change control, and notice provisions when relevant. Separate extracted text from commentary. Do not provide legal advice or conclude enforceability.

## Output Guidance

The review packet should include clause matrix, risk flags, missing/ambiguous terms, source references, confidence, and attorney-review questions. Recommend human legal review before negotiation, signature, compliance action, or reliance on any interpretation.
