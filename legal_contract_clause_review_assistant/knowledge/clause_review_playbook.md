# Contract Clause Review Playbook

Use this guidance as local retrieval context for clause review and playbook comparison.

## Clause Evidence

- Extract only clauses grounded in the supplied contract text, annotations, or configured playbook.
- Preserve clause text snippets, section names, source file references, and confidence warnings.
- Keep privilege, confidentiality, and deal-sensitive data local and redacted in logs.

## Review Checks

- Flag missing governing law, assignment, termination, indemnity, limitation of liability, renewal, payment, data handling, audit, and change-of-control terms when relevant.
- Distinguish clause absence from OCR failure, unreadable pages, or taxonomy mismatch.
- Compare against the playbook by issue area and explain gaps without giving legal advice.

## Tool Boundaries

- OCR, clause extraction, and taxonomy mapping assist attorney intake only.
- Do not rewrite, approve, or reject contract terms without human legal review.
