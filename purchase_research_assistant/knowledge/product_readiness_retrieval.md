# Purchase Research Product Readiness Retrieval

Use this checklist when reviewing the generated purchase packet.

- Is the purchase type, item or trip, budget, location or route, timing, and priority set explicit?
- Are local documents identified by path, hash, extraction method, and source reference?
- Are public queries privacy-safe and free of raw private document text?
- Are observed prices, availability, policies, fees, dates, and source statuses preserved deterministically?
- Are total-cost components separated from asking price and marked unknown when absent?
- Are blocked, stale, conflicting, login-required, robots, and CAPTCHA sources recorded as warnings?
- Do RAG citations identify the retrieved guidance or approved local document?
- Does the recommendation use only the five bounded labels and show confidence, risks, gaps, and next steps?
- Does the final report clearly block buying, booking, payment, offers, applications, and seller/provider contact?
