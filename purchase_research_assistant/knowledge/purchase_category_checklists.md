# Purchase Category Checklists

Use category-specific retrieval to focus the review without assuming facts.

| Category | Price and fee checks | Risk and policy checks |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Property | taxes, insurance, closing, HOA, utilities, financing | inspection, title, flood, lease, occupancy, maintenance |
| Rental property | rent, deposit, utilities, application, move-in, recurring fees | lease, renewal, repairs, habitability, cancellation, landlord evidence |
| Car | asking price, dealer fees, tax, registration, financing, insurance, maintenance | VIN, title, accident, recall, inspection, warranty, return policy |
| Airline ticket | fare, taxes, baggage, seat, change, cancellation, exchange-rate fees | fare rules, schedule, connection, refund, credit, entry requirements |
| Custom | quoted price, delivery, subscription, support, taxes, recurring cost | warranty, return, safety, compatibility, seller identity, service terms |

If a field is not present in the local packet or a cited public source, mark it as missing. A checklist item is not evidence by itself.
