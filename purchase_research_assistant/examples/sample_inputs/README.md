# Purchase Research Sample Inputs

The sample packet models a buyer screening three synthetic single-family homes
in ZIP code 03755. It is safe for fake/offline runs and demonstrates hard
constraint checks, five-year known-cost comparison, disclosure triage, local
evidence retrieval, missing-evidence gates, and review-only output.

The candidate records are snapshots, not live listings. Prices, taxes,
insurance, condition notes, and availability must be independently verified
before any real-world use.

Replace these files with approved local evidence such as listings, quotes, fee schedules, inspection notes, fare rules, rental disclosures, warranty documents, or comparison spreadsheets. Do not include secrets or documents that the configured runtime is not authorized to process.
