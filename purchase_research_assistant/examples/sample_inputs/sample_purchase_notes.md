# Synthetic purchase notes

The bundled goal is to find one single-family house with at least 3 bedrooms in ZIP code 03755.

Three synthetic candidate snapshots are supplied for deterministic comparison.
They are not live listings and are not proof of availability or condition.
Public research and a human reviewer must verify the ZIP code, property type,
bedroom count, asking price, taxes, insurance, listing freshness, title, and
material inspection or condition risks before treating one as a viable option.

The notes are intentionally incomplete so the assistant preserves financing,
inspection, title, utilities, insurance, and resale evidence gaps and keeps the
recommendation review-only.
