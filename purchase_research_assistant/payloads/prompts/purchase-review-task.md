# Purchase Research Review Task

## Goal

Review a purchase research packet and prepare bounded findings for human decision-making.

## Instructions

- Use only the supplied deterministic evidence, retrieved local context, and cited public-source records.
- Distinguish observed facts, conflicts, assumptions, unknowns, and next evidence requests.
- Check price, total cost, availability, policy, condition, safety, flexibility, and alternatives according to the purchase type.
- Keep the recommendation constrained to `buy`, `consider`, `wait`, `avoid`, or `insufficient_evidence`.
- Preserve source references and retrieval timestamps for volatile facts.

## Restrictions

- Do not invent prices, availability, policies, fees, condition, legal status, safety, or travel requirements.
- Do not expose private local evidence in public queries.
- Do not buy, book, pay, submit an offer or application, or contact a seller/provider.
