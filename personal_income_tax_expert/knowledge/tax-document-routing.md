# Tax Document Routing

## W-2

- Route wage amount to Form 1040 line 1z.
- Route federal income tax withheld to payments and withholding.
- Ask about additional W-2 forms before treating wage income as complete.

## 1099-INT

- Route taxable interest to Form 1040 line 2b.
- Ask whether Schedule B is needed when interest totals, foreign accounts, or
  special cases are present.

## 1099-R and 401(k)

- Route gross retirement distributions to Form 1040 retirement distribution
  review.
- Route taxable amount to line 4b when supported by the form.
- Ask about rollover, Roth, early distribution, and exception codes before
  calculating additional tax.

## Other Common Forms

- 1099-DIV and 1099-B need dividend/capital gain handling.
- 1098 can support mortgage interest review when itemizing.
- 1095-A can trigger premium tax credit reconciliation.
- 1099-NEC can imply Schedule C and self-employment tax.
