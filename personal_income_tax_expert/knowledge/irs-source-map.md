# IRS Source Map

Use current IRS sources as the authoritative references for federal individual
income tax form structure and common source documents.

## Core References

- Form 1040: https://www.irs.gov/Form1040
- Publication 17: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p17
- Form 1099-INT: https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-1099-int
- Form 1099-R: https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-1099-r

## 2025 Draft Packet Defaults

- Standard deduction: Single or married filing separately, 15750.
- Standard deduction: Married filing jointly or qualifying surviving spouse, 31500.
- Standard deduction: Head of household, 23625.

The blueprint uses these values only for a draft packet. If tax law, filing
status, age/blindness, dependency, itemized deductions, or other facts differ,
the advisor must ask for clarification.
