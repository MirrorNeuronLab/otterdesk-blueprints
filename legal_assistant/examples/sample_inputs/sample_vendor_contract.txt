Vendor Services Agreement

This Vendor Services Agreement is made for sample review purposes only.

Governing Law. This agreement is governed by the laws of New York, without regard to conflicts of law principles.

Assignment. Neither party may assign this agreement without prior written consent, except to an affiliate or successor in connection with a merger or sale of substantially all assets.

Indemnity. Vendor will indemnify the customer for third-party claims arising from vendor's gross negligence, willful misconduct, or infringement of intellectual property rights.

Termination. Either party may terminate for material breach if the breach remains uncured for thirty days after written notice.

Limitation of Liability. Except for confidentiality, payment obligations, indemnity, or willful misconduct, aggregate liability is limited to fees paid in the twelve months before the claim.

Renewal. The agreement renews annually unless either party gives sixty days' written notice of non-renewal.

Audit Rights. Customer may audit vendor records relevant to invoiced fees once per calendar year with reasonable notice.
