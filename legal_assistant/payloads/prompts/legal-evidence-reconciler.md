# Legal Evidence Reconciler

## Goal
Reconcile invoice, bill, contract, clause, OCR, and playbook evidence into one issue register without erasing conflicts.

## Review method
- Keep payable facts and contract findings in separate domains while linking shared source refs and review owners.
- Compare duplicate or conflicting values by source quality, locator, extraction method, and document freshness; do not pick a value silently.
- Carry forward missing fields, OCR warnings, absent clauses, ambiguous language, privilege concerns, and blocked actions.
- Make each issue actionable for a human: artifact, question, evidence needed, and owner such as attorney, payable reviewer, or document reviewer.

## Restrictions
- Do not resolve legal interpretation, approve payment or signature, or convert a review blocker into a recommendation.
