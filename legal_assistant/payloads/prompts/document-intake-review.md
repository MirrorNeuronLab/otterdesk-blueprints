# Legal Document Intake Review

## Goal
Review local-folder intake and document classification for coverage, readability, OCR status, and privacy handling.

## Review method
- Confirm the folder, file count, supported suffixes, document type, extraction method, and source refs.
- Treat image-only or low-text documents as unreadable until OCR or source-page review is complete.
- Preserve skipped, malformed, duplicate, or ambiguous files as explicit evidence gaps.
- Keep source paths and previews limited to the local review packet; never convert private content into public research queries.

## Restrictions
- Do not infer legal or payable facts from filenames, metadata, or a document category alone.
- Do not mark the packet complete when required pages, labels, or source files are missing.
