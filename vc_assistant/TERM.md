# Terms And Review Boundaries

This blueprint produces score-only startup analysis reports for human review.

The workflow may create many reports. Root-level index and coverage files are intended to help reviewers navigate large batches without turning the workflow into an automated investment filter.

Platform human-control approval values are generic workflow controls and must not be interpreted as company-level investment labels.

It must not:

- issue invest, pass, watch, or reject decisions,
- send confidential document excerpts to public search,
- claim that heuristic scores are valuations,
- replace analyst, legal, tax, or investment committee review.

Public research may use privacy-safe public descriptors such as company name, product category, domain, and non-confidential public claims.

Crunchbase, LinkedIn, and similar public profile sites may require login, rate-limit requests, or block automated access. The workflow must record those outcomes and stop; it must not use proxy rotation, CAPTCHA solving, stealth plugins, or credentialed access unless a human configures an approved enterprise connector later.
