# Property Deal Research Playbook

Use this guidance as local retrieval context for real-estate opportunity review.

## Evidence Grounding

- Separate ZIP-level context, property-specific facts, broker notes, financing constraints, and memory-derived historical signals.
- Preserve source refs for critical facts that drive ranking decisions.
- Treat simulated market history and benchmark outcomes as review aids, not appraisals.

## Review Checks

- Flag stale comps, missing inspection data, financing gaps, flood or insurance risk, tenant concentration, rent-growth uncertainty, and seller-motivation assumptions.
- Compare optimized working-memory decisions against all-context and oracle-style benchmarks.
- Keep bid, watchlist, and pass labels review-only unless a human decision is recorded.

## Tool Boundaries

- Working-memory retrieval and ranking tools should surface evidence and uncertainty.
- Do not infer confidential broker details into public research queries or external outputs.
