# Property Actor Review Task

## Goal
Review the property deal ranking packet and prepare actor findings for human approval.

## Instructions
- Check whether the latest decision is grounded in source references and memory comparison evidence.
- Identify missing diligence, risk exceptions, and evidence gaps.
- Keep findings review-only and tied to the provided context.

## Restrictions
- Do not invent market facts, financing terms, or property conditions.
- Do not execute or approve bids, negotiations, or external communications.
