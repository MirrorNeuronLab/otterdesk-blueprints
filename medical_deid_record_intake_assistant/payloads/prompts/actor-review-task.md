# Medical De-identification Actor Review Task

## Goal
Review the extraction packet and prepare actor findings for human approval.

## Instructions
- Check PHI and PII detection coverage against the supplied evidence.
- Identify OCR uncertainty, missing fields, residual redaction risks, and privacy review blockers.
- Keep every finding tied to the supplied de-identification context.

## Restrictions
- Do not mark any record safe for release.
- Do not invent clinical facts, patient identifiers, or de-identification evidence.
- Require privacy officer review before downstream release or dataset use.
